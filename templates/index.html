Managing your referral base matters

The average surgical yield for General Surgery clinics is 17%. That is, fewer than 2 in 10 patients seen by surgeons actually result in surgery. That number is even worse in surgical sub-specialties, like Spine or Orthopedic clinics.

Such a low yield is bad for the patient and the provider.

Maintaining a strong referral base and rewarding the best referring physicians and practices is the most effective way to improve your surgical yield.

Let us help you manage and grow a healthy referral base.

Our product

  • Track your referring physicians and practices
  • Get insights into the best and worst behavors in your referral base
  • Get insights into new entrants into your referral base and big shifts
  • Track appropriateness and complexity of referrals
  • Get insights into who is adding the most value and who is costing your practice a lot of time
  • Get recommendations and easy tools to strengthen good relationships and phase out bad relationships
